Why you should visit Siargao

Known as the "Surfing Capital of the Philippines". Siargao is a dream tropical destination for both local and international travelers and a perfect place to surf. It is not only for those who want to have their honeymoon but also for those who loves to take their adventure
to the next level.

Where you can go

Siargao is not just Siargao. There are a lot of places you can go in Siargao. Hop on to the 3 famous islands (Daku, Naked and Guyam Island). Explore Sohoton Cove, go to Maasin River or watch and surf at Cloud 9.

What you can do

Aside from the common 'walking at the seaside', you can also experience doing a lot of water activities such as
diving, swimming and surfing. Enjoy hopping on different island and experience a wonderful land tour. Take pictures
at Burgos Coconut Road and ride a motorbike around the island.
